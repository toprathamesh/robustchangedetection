Automated Data Acquisition

Automatically download and preprocess Sentinel-2 and Landsat imagery for your areas of interest with cloud masking and radiometric normalization.

Deep Learning Detection

State-of-the-art U-Net architecture for pixel-wise semantic segmentation and accurate change detection with confidence scoring.

Smart Alerts

Get notified via email when significant changes are detected in your monitored areas with customizable thresholds and frequency controls.

Interactive Mapping

Draw areas of interest on an interactive map, visualize change results, and explore temporal patterns with intuitive controls.
